Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Oref Volunteer Bot ("the Service") is designed to facilitate communication between volunteers and individuals requesting assistance during emergencies, community activities, and humanitarian operations.

2. Information We Collect

When interacting with the Service through WhatsApp, Telegram, or other communication channels, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information
  • Phone number
  • Messages submitted to the Service
  • Location information voluntarily provided by users
  • Volunteer registration details
  • Assistance requests and status updates

3. How We Use Information

Information collected is used solely for:

  • Providing volunteer coordination services
  • Managing emergency assistance requests
  • Communicating with users regarding their requests
  • Improving the Service
  • Maintaining system security and reliability

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information may only be shared with authorized volunteers, administrators, or emergency coordinators when necessary to fulfill a user's request.

5. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with applicable legal obligations.

7. User Rights

Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information by contacting the Service administrator.

8. Third-Party Services

The Service utilizes third-party platforms including WhatsApp, Meta Platforms, Inc., Telegram, and cloud infrastructure providers. Users are also subject to the privacy policies of those services.

9. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
